What We Offer

Piscis Aquarium is an indoor experience of underwater sightseeing. Learn and meet over 5000 various marine animals. Witness the ocean like never before through our shows and attractions. Get up close and personal with your favorite marine animals, maybe even feed them too! Fun for the whole family, perfect for a holiday getaway.
